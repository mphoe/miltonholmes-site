Glass Balls and Skyscrapers

The Mission

You have a huge collection of identical glass balls, and you want to find the height you can drop the balls from without them breaking. Fortunately you live in a skyscraper and you have access to a window at each floor. Balls dropped from below their limit will be fine, but drop a ball from too high up and it will shatter.

Goals

  1. Find out the highest floor you can drop the balls from without them breaking. Try to break the fewest balls possible. What was your technique?
  2. Now try to aim for the fewest attempts possible, it doesn't matter how many you break. How have you changed your technique?
